Vintage Raketa
(unknown ref.)

Purchased 19-08-2017

I'm a big fan of this watch, it's the first vintage watch I've ever owned, and its a delight. To be honest, the build quality isn't the best, I'm pretty sure the internal movement is not secure, as I can hear an audible thud when I shake the watch, and the crystal is acrylic, so I'm constantly afraid of smashing it, but it's a beautiful watch, and one that I love a lot. I purchased it to commemorate my leaving General Assembly London.
