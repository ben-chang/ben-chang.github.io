Seiko 5
SNXS79K

Purchased 04-07-2017

This was definetly the watch that got me into watches. My first automatic watch, and a damn fine one if I had to say so myself. It's a beautifully made watch, and definetly my most accurate mechanical watch. The hardlex unfortunately scratches a bit easy, and the case isn't the most resiliant, but for under $100 I can't complain. However....I can complain about Seiko's entery level folding bracelet. It is fairly comfortable, but man the pain I had adjusting it, and its tendency to cut things with its scapel sharp edges (it cut open a pocket on my Barbour jacket) makes it a testy bracelet to own.
