Purchased 18-10-2017

This watch is definitively the darling of the watch geek world for being such an inexpensive (relatively) , but simultaneously stunning piece of watch making. I wavered on purchasing it, but ultimately caved for the reason that it is such a durable piece. I bought it in commemoration for my first real job as an engineer (albiet before my first paycheck which in hindsight was definetly way too irresponsible). I've put it on a Strapcode Super Oyster, which I have to admit was a bit difficult to size. However, once adjusted properly, the watch and strap feel solid. Everytime I see it, I marvel in its handsome good looks, and when I wear it the watch truly feels like the first quality time piece I've owned. It is impressively heavy though (153 g).
